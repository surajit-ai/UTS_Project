1. The 'utsonmoblie' application is developed in-house by Center for Railway Information Systems(CRIS).

2.The 'utsonmobile' application is avalilable for Android,Windows and ios based smartphones. User can download this application from Google play store,Windows app store and Apply store respectively,free of cost.

3. The pasanger first will get registered by providing his/her mobile number name,password,gender and date of birth.

4. Upon successful registration, Railway Wallet (R-WALLET) will be created automaticaly with zero balance to the pasanger. There will be no exter cost for creating

5. R-WALLET can be rechanged either at any of the UTS counter of through recharge option availble on the "https://www.utsonmobile.indianrail.gov" website.

6. Currently, paperless Ticket can be booked for journey Ticket, Season Ticket and platform ticket. platform ticket booking is aiiowed for some selected station only.

7. In case internet connection of the mobile is not up, then ticket booking is not allowed.

8. No advance ticket booking is allowed i.e. the journey date will always be current date.
